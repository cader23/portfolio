About Me

Hi, I’m Sahejpreet Singh, a passionate Data Analyst with a unique journey. At the age of 20, I successfully cleared my Class 10 exams, proving that determination and resilience matter more than timing. My inspiring story has been featured in Times of India, ABP Majha, Red FM Canada, and Prime Asia TV.

I have been awarded and honored by Malaika Arora, Eknath Shinde (Chief Minister of Maharashtra), Mid-Day Newspaper, and Navbharat for my academic achievements and unique path. These recognitions motivate me to continue pushing boundaries in education, technology, and innovation.

To strengthen my expertise, I earned 28+ certifications across Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Business Communication, and more — from institutions like Google, IBM, Infosys, and edX. I specialize in building dashboards, automation workflows, and AI-driven solutions that turn raw data into meaningful insights.

Beyond academics, I am also an entrepreneur, working on AI assistants, automation tools, and business ventures across Canada and India. My ultimate mission is to combine data, technology, and innovation to create solutions that empower individuals, businesses, and communities worldwide.
